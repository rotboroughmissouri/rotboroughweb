What's B.F.F.?

Boy’s Friendship Foundation was founded to help the children of Rotborough who may be struggling with loneliness, bullying, or general social issues during their time recovering from serious illnesses in nearby hospitals! Since 1982 our goal has been to become the difference between a speedy happy recovery and the “alternative”. Giving your little one the joy every child has the right to experience in their early years, and you the peace of mind knowing your child is in good hands! But since then we’ve expanded to offer more ways to help give back to our community.

Local Sponsors:

Through our local sponsors – NXHealth, Growing Minds Learning Center, and The Kidz 4 Mayor Gray After School Initiative, we are able to provide top of the line help for your child, through education, health care or just fun! Last year alone we were able to offer Saint Mary's Baptist Holy Redemption Regional Medical Health Memorial Children’s Hospital free RageBoy’s and school supplies, courtesy of Rotplay! (Keep your eyes peeled for their next game “My Pet Boy" in stores soon!)

How can you help?

There are many ways to help us with our cause and the many boys and girls we work with. One way is by spreading the word. If you know someone who’s child is affected by a terminal illness or a family in need of assistance, tell them help is available and give them our contact details! Another way is to make a charitable donation of any amount. A little goes a long way. Another way is through our Youth and Adult Volunteer programs!

Volunteer Programs:

We’ve made it easier for you to help out through our Youth and Adult Volunteer programs! Every Sunday (or any free day you have in your week) we offer community outreach programs to locals in Rotborough! From after school programs to disaster relief efforts. We now have over 150 volunteers donating countless hours to our cause! And to them we are eternally grateful.
