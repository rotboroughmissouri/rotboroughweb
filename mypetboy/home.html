Inspired by the heartwarming tale of Boy, the miracle cow born with no front legs, Rotplay is proud to present the Rotborough icon’s first video game experience! You’ll take Boy home as a young calf, and raise him as your very own pet. Teach him to walk, dress him up, and feed him yummy treats! Be careful though - Boy is quite naughty, so keep him out of trouble and away from drugs and alcohol. Incase he does get mischievious, with Rage Boy Face recognition technology, you’ll be able to scold and punish the badly behaved bovine with your own voice! Will you raise Boy into an upstanding citizen, or will you watch him turn into a drunken derelict? Truthfully, we don’t care, as long as you have fun!
